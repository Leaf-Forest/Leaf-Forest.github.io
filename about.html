About

My story.

Mink Photography was inspired after a walk in the woods. I had my camera with me, ready to take some pictures. At some point I came across a small, brown and furry animal. We stared at each other for a while, neither of us daring to move. After a few moments I saw an opportunity and snapped a few pictures of it. Amazingly, it was a great photo; the animal was perched perfectly on log. Later I went back home and did some research. I found out that the mysterious animal was actually an American Mink.

The next day I discovered an old html test file. It had only two pictures displayed, so I decided to add a few more.

Mink Photography was born.



Why a website?

I love nature. I love the trees, the plants, animals, the water, all of it. Takeing pictures gives me the chance to try to capture even a tiny bit of nature's awe. A website enables me to share some of my awe with others, and learn in the process.
